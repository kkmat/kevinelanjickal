Senior executive and entrepreneur based in Kuwait, currently serving as Deputy Managing Director of Nexus Engineering General Trading & Contracting Co. W.L.L. Deeply involved in the strategic management, financial structuring, and operational execution of multi-disciplinary businesses spanning civil and mechanical contracting, oil & gas services, scaffolding and formwork, aviation ground handling, scrap trading, and real-estate investments across Kuwait, Oman, and the UAE.

Actively focused on professionalizing and scaling family-owned and partner-structured enterprises through stronger governance, digital transformation, and data-driven decision-making. Expanded Nexus operations internationally with Nexus Construction LLC in Oman and Medlar Construction LLC in Abu Dhabi.

Currently pursuing a Doctor of Business Administration (DBA). PMP certified with an MSc in Advanced Engineering Management from the University of Birmingham and a BEng in Mechanical Engineering from Swansea University. Harvard Business School CORe credential holder. Associate member of IMechE, member of APM, and registered Mechanical Engineer with the Kuwait Society of Engineers.

Duke of Edinburgh Gold Award — South Africa Residential Project

As charity leader for the Kuwait English School International Award Gold group (2013/2014), led a residential project in Paarl, South Africa supporting the Khanyise Day Care Centre — a facility caring for 32 children aged 0-6 in severely cramped conditions, where a temporary structure had been destroyed by recent storms.

Raised £1,300 GBP (~R22,000) through student-driven fundraising. Funds provided staple food supplies for a week, electricity, educational materials, toys, and books — plus R10,620 toward the Centre's building fund for reconstruction. The team purchased teddy bears for every child; some had never owned a toy before.

Organised and delivered a full day programme including picnic meals, sing-alongs, aerobics, arts and crafts, and circle time activities. An experience that remains one of the most meaningful chapters of my life.

Lean Construction in Kuwaiti Oil & Gas

MSc Dissertation · University of Birmingham
Advanced Engineering Management, 2019
Download PDF →
This dissertation investigates the application of lean construction principles within Kuwait's oil and gas sector. Through qualitative research and industry interviews, the study identifies key barriers to lean adoption and proposes a framework for implementation tailored to the region's unique project delivery challenges.

Development of Printable RFID Device

BEng Research Project · Swansea University
Mechanical Engineering, 2018
Download PDF →
This project explores the feasibility of manufacturing RFID devices using printable conductive inks and flexible substrates. The research covers antenna design optimisation, material characterisation, and performance testing of printed prototypes against conventional RFID tags.
