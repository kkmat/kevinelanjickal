Duke of Edinburgh Gold Award — South Africa Residential Project

As charity leader for the Kuwait English School International Award Gold group (2013/2014), led a residential project in Paarl, South Africa supporting the Khanyise Day Care Centre — a facility caring for 32 children aged 0-6 in severely cramped conditions, where a temporary structure had been destroyed by recent storms.

Raised £1,300 GBP (~R22,000) through student-driven fundraising. Funds provided staple food supplies for a week, electricity, educational materials, toys, and books — plus R10,620 toward the Centre's building fund for reconstruction. The team purchased teddy bears for every child; some had never owned a toy before.

Organised and delivered a full day programme including picnic meals, sing-alongs, aerobics, arts and crafts, and circle time activities. An experience that remains one of the most meaningful chapters of my life.
