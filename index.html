About me

Senior executive and entrepreneur based in Kuwait, currently serving as Deputy Managing Director of Nexus Engineering General Trading & Contracting Co. W.L.L. Deeply involved in the strategic management, financial structuring, and operational execution of multi-disciplinary businesses spanning civil and mechanical contracting, oil & gas services, scaffolding and formwork, aviation ground handling, scrap trading, and real-estate investments across Kuwait, Oman, and the UAE.

Actively focused on professionalizing and scaling family-owned and partner-structured enterprises through stronger governance, digital transformation, and data-driven decision-making. Leading initiatives in ERP implementation, financial modeling, AI-assisted analytics, and IT infrastructure modernization. Expanded Nexus operations internationally with Nexus Construction LLC in Oman and Medlar Construction LLC in Abu Dhabi.

Currently pursuing a Doctor of Business Administration (DBA). PMP certified with an MSc in Advanced Engineering Management from the University of Birmingham and a BEng in Mechanical Engineering from Swansea University. Harvard Business School CORe credential holder. Associate member of IMechE, member of APM, and registered Mechanical Engineer with the Kuwait Society of Engineers.
